HANGARX SERIES — MASTER VISUAL STYLE GUIDE
Copy this entire block into any new thread to maintain
100% visual consistency across all images and comics.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

━━━ ART STYLE CORE ━━━
Realistic cinematic painterly digital art.
Photorealistic digital painting — like a Hollywood 
blockbuster movie poster. High detail, volumetric 
3D lighting, painterly brushwork visible in midtones.
Style DNA: Top Gun: Maverick × Blade Runner 2049 × 
The Dark Knight × Mad Max: Fury Road.

STRICTLY: NOT comic book. NOT cartoon. NOT animated.
NOT Guardians of the Galaxy. NOT Marvel superhero 
costume design. NO ink outlines. NO halftone dots.
NO flat cel shading. NO exaggerated proportions.

━━━ LIGHTING SETUP ━━━
Primary: Warm amber-orange sunset/dusk rim lighting
from behind and above — golden hour at HangarX base.
Secondary: Teal-cyan glow from holographic tech 
displays, data visualizations, and Vector core.
Villain accent: Crimson red (#CC0000) energy from
The Algorithm — dark, threatening, emanating from above.
Style: Dramatic chiaroscuro — deep shadows, bright 
highlights, atmospheric dust haze. Lens flares on 
key tech elements. Volumetric god rays through
industrial hangar windows.

━━━ COLOR PALETTE ━━━
Background (deep space): #0a0a1a  (near-black navy)
Hero light (warm):        #F5C518  (amber gold)
Hero light (secondary):   #FF8C00  (orange gold)
Hero tech accent:         #00C9C8  (teal cyan)
Hero tech secondary:      #00E5FF  (electric cyan)
Villain energy:           #CC0000  (deep crimson)
Villain energy hot:       #FF2200  (bright crimson)
Data / code streams:      #00FF41  (matrix green)
Typography primary:       #FFFFFF  (white)
Typography accent:        #F5C518  (amber gold headers)
Vulnerability text:       #CC0000  (crimson red)
Card borders:             #00C9C8  (teal cyan)

━━━ ENVIRONMENT / BACKGROUND ━━━
Setting: HangarX — abandoned military air base 
converted into a high-tech resistance operations 
center. Weathered corrugated metal hangars, heavy 
industrial lighting, aircraft and vehicles in 
background. Sky: dramatic dusk gradient — amber 
orange at horizon fading to deep purple-blue at top.
Atmospheric dust and smoke particles. Neon signage
reading "HANGARX" in teal-cyan or amber. Holographic
data displays floating in air. Distant mountains 
silhouetted against sunset.

━━━ CHARACTER DESIGN RULES ━━━
Body: Realistic human proportions — athletic/fit but 
NOT exaggerated superhero muscles. Real-world tactical 
military aesthetic. Think Delta Force × Silicon Valley 
× CIA field operative.
Base outfit: Dark form-fitting tactical suit/gear 
(dark charcoal, near-black). Each character has 
role-specific gear overlaid on top:
  — SecOps: Full balaclava + tactical helmet + visor
  — Data Engineer: Tactical vest + holographic network graphs
  — Data Scientist: White lab coat + AR goggles + drones
  — QA Engineer: Golden amber tactical armor + net launcher
  — Helix: Form-fitting dark suit + "HELIX" chest badge + holo-display
  — Developer: Tactical jacket + teal holographic keyboard
  — Solutions Architect: Dark suit + teal circuit pattern lines + blueprints
  — SRE: Heavy hazmat-military hybrid gear + industrial tools
  — Security Auditor: Dark business suit + tie + classified tablet
  — Marketing Expert: Business-casual tactical jacket + teal broadcast megaphone
Lighting on characters: Warm amber rim light from 
behind, teal fill from tech props, subtle crimson 
threat light from distant Algorithm presence.
Expressions: Determined, haunted, focused — NOT 
smiling hero poses. Carry weight of their mission.

━━━ POSTER / SINGLE CHARACTER IMAGE STYLE ━━━
Format: 2:3 ratio (portrait) — standard movie poster
Composition: Character centered or slightly off-center,
filling 70% of frame height. Background environment 
visible behind. Title/text at bottom third.
Text hierarchy: 
  1. Studio line (small, top)
  2. Tagline (medium, below studio)  
  3. CHARACTER NAME (massive, bold, lower third — 
     distressed metallic gold letters with teal glow)
  4. Role subtitle (medium, below name)
  5. Series branding (small, bottom bar)
Typography: Distressed military stencil font for 
character name. Clean bold sans-serif for supporting 
text. All amber/gold or white.

━━━ TEAM ENSEMBLE POSTER STYLE ━━━
Format: 16:9 ratio (wide cinematic)
Composition: Characters arranged in a dramatic arc —
central lead character (Helix) front and center, 
taller characters behind, flanking members on sides.
Each character holding/using their signature prop.
Depth: foreground characters fully lit, background 
characters silhouetted or partially obscured by haze.
Background: Full HangarX base, sunset sky, aircraft.
Text: Series title bold across bottom, tagline above.

━━━ DOSSIER PROFILE CARD STYLE ━━━
Format: Two cards side-by-side, 4:3 ratio total
Each card structure (top to bottom):
  [CHARACTER PORTRAIT — upper 60% of card]
    Cinematic portrait, 3/4 angle, amber-lit, 
    HangarX hangar window background with sunset
  [DOSSIER TEXT PANEL — lower 40% of card]
    Dark navy #0a0a1a background
    Teal-cyan (#00C9C8) card border, rounded corners
Card text layout:
  — CODENAME: [NAME] | ROLE: [ROLE TITLE]       (amber)
  — TIER: [X] | THREAT TO ALGORITHM: [bar] [level] 
  — STRENGTHS: [list with / separators]          (white)
  — VULNERABILITIES: [list]                      (crimson)
  — ALGORITHM EXPLOIT: ⚠ [NAME] — [description] (crimson)
Threat bars: ████████████ CRITICAL (full red)
             ████████░░░░ HIGH     (orange)
Overlays: CLASSIFIED stamp in red, REDACTED black 
bars over sensitive sections, HangarX logo watermark.

━━━ COMIC PAGE STYLE ━━━
Format: 2:3 portrait (768×1152px), 5 panels vertical
Panel style: Thick black gutters between panels.
Art: Jim Lee precision linework × Todd McFarlane 
dramatic shadows × Alex Ross painterly realism.
Hero color: Teal-cyan accents on all protagonist tech
Villain color: Crimson energy, dark red lightning
Data/code: Matrix green (#00FF41) binary streams
Caption boxes: Yellow with black bold text
Speech bubbles: White with black text
Sound effects: Large bold distressed lettering
Panel variety: Mix of close-up portraits, wide 
establishing shots, and action-angle dynamic panels.
Backgrounds always fully rendered — never empty.
